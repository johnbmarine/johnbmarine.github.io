Original Character Series: Top Diva

Welcome to my Top Diva story! Allow me to introduce you to this set of characters set across multiple storylines.

In an effort to promote tourism of Diva State, a unique promotion was set up- a fighting tournament. Women between the ages of 18 and 30 all fought in worldwide competitions until a select few would be invited to live in and compete in Diva State.

While most have simply enjoyed the experience of participating and competing, some who did not qualify as finalists returned home had lots of stories to tell to friends and family. Some others, felt the desire to sabotage the tournament and ruin the lives of the tournament finalists. One such dejected non-finalist was instrumental in banding together some non-finalists as well as some locals to create chaos. Factions were formed, and many different random fights and vandalism ensued. Certain parts of Diva State were claimed by factions to ensure Diva State would be brought to its knees.

One non-finalist actually studied the patterns of violence and chaos and is trying to be a whistleblower trying to expose the evil plot. While the finalists have little idea that they are potentially threatened, they band together despite their differences to try to restore order to Diva State.

In addition to the madness of the factions, some Independent fighters also have their say in the control of Diva State.
