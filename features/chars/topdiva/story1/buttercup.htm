Buttercup Bonnet

* Full Name: Buttercup Daisy Bonnet

* From: St. Louis, Missouri, USA

* 24 years old

* Private Investigator, (sometimes) a gardener

* American hertiage, and part Irish


Biography

Being named Buttercup Daisy Bonnet means one must love flowers. And actually, she does. Buttercup did not make the finalist crew for the first season of Top Diva. However, she plays an important role in how the story of Story 1 is played out.

Personality

Buttercup can be rather shy a person, but she is not shy about trying to expose a plot regarding the tournament. She failed to become one of the eight finalists of the tournament. Buttercup has utter disdain for one non-finalist named Carmel Henderson. She plays as a decoy or a sting operator to pretend being one of the Jealous Girls or any of the factions trying to tear apart Diva State. Her clear goal is to try to inform the finalists that they are targets to Carmel and her many henchwomen.

Friendships and Rivalries

Buttercup has healthy dislike for Carmel Henderson, and she would love to defeat Carmel any chance she gets.

Fashion

Buttercup has a fondness for high-waist style. To that end, her outfit primarly consists of a button-down shirt and some high-waist bottoms. She has some variations of the look to get some diversity going.

OUTFIT 1:

Fighting

Buttercup can be more nonchalant a fighter. She seems casually serious, and it shows. Buttercup often feels she can have her opponent flirt with her, and then lay down some serious offense.
