Carmel Henderson

* Nickname: (she's called the "Absolute Villain" of Top Diva)

* From: Washington, D.C., USA

* 19 years old

* Fashion model

* 5 feet, 7 inches tall

* American heritage


Biography

Carmel is the villain of Top Diva. She is trying to sabotage the first-ever all-female fighting tournament held in Diva State.

Personality

Carmel Henderson was among many women trying to become part of the field of finalists for the first-ever all-female fighting tournament. When she failed to qualify, she was bitterly disappointed. Carmel felt a number of women were more talented than the finalists. So rather than try again in a future tournament, she decided to enlist a number of factions to try to take over Diva State. Collectively, these girls were known as the "Jealous Girls" to many people.

Fighting

Carmel is more petty than a talented fighter. Her method of getting in peoples' heads is to do things most people would consider disgraceful or classless. In fact, she even enlisted "Jealous Girls" to provide melee attacks to protect her.

Specifications: Carmel is a non-playable character and is not one of the finalists in this tournament. However, her statistics are as follows:

Agility (quickness and evasion)
Stamina (how well can damage be sustained)
Mass (more = heavier; less = lighter)
