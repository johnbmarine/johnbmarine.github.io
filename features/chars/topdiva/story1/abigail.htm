Abigail "A.J." Garcia

* Nicknamed: Abbie, AJ, Salvadoran Sweetheart

* From: New York City, New York, USA

* 21 years old

* College Student

* 5 feet, 4 inches tall

* Salvadoran-American


Biography

Coming from a major city, one young lady knows the importance of standing out among others. Enter one Abigail Jessica Garcia. She is commonly referred to as "Abbie" or "AJ." AJ describes herself as an absolute girly-girl. AJ does not let the fact she is from a major city alter her personality or train of thought. Even AJ understands her limits. She is a college student of St. John's University in New York City studying Mass Communications. Both of her parents are United States citizens from their native El Salvador. Abbie celebrates her Salvadoran heritage as well as her American heritage.

Personality

As far as being a person goes, Abigail is one of the sweetest and most humble persons one will ever meet. She was well taught by her Salvadoran parents to be humble and kind. She smiles often, as if her smile is surgically attached to her face. She can not stand being around mean or sarcastic people. Abbie's passion to make herself and others smile is important to her, as she feels this world can be so cruel and in need of some sort of positive reinforcement.

As far as being an "absolute girly-girl," Abigail loves almost everything most her age enjoy. She is an avid shopper and loves to have fun. AJ even enjoys cooking. Even this big city girl has a girl-next-door personality and never tries to be a drama queen.

Abigail is also aware that she is not slim. In fact, AJ still considers herself beautiful even if she is not on the slim and slender side. There was a point in her High School life where she was not happy with her body appearance. While AJ was never obese, she does regularly try to maintain her weight. She does not consider herself chubby despite being at the high end of being petite.


Fashion

Abigail cares about fashion, but she is not into any trends or feel she has to dress like everyone else to be accepted. In other words, she does not care if her style is years behind the rest of the world. AJ simply wears what she feels most comfortable in. Some people feel a lot of her outfits seem too childish to wear for a 21-year old.

OUTFIT 1:
Abigail's main outfit consists of a cropped denim jean bolero, a satin camisole, mid-calf length culotte pants, and a pair of canvas sneakers. The culotte pants are pleated and are skirt-like.

OUTFIT 2:
Abigail wears an adorable outfit consisting of a cap-sleeve T-shirt, a dark gray sundress, black leggings, and black ballet flats.

OUTFIT 3:
Being a bit daring, Abigail wears an outfit consisting of a cropped top, jeans cuffed at around mid-calf, and paired with some casual metallic sandals.


OUTFIT 4:
For nights out, Abigail wears a satin kimono dress along with some metallic sandals.

Fighting

As a part of a fighting tournament, Abigail is seen as being overall average despite her heavy body set.

Specifications:

Offense (punches, kicks, and throws)
Agility (quickness and evasion)
Stamina (how well can damage be sustained)
Mass (more = heavier; less = lighter)

Friendships and Rivalries

Abigail is best friends with Candy Watanabe. The two got to enjoy being around each other during qualifying trials. She also developed friendships with the likes of Suzie St. Marie after being concerned about Suzie's mental health issues. Despite the healthy love for Candy, Candy is considered Abigail's primary rival.

Trivia

* Abigail's color combination of pink/white/brown was semi-inspired by Neapolitan ice cream.

* Because this character was one of my creations from around 2008, the culotte pants worn by Abigail are gaucho pants.
