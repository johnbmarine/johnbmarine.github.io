Marisol Castillo

* From: Ibiza Town, Ibiza, Spain

* Nicknamed: Original bell bottoms girl

* 23 years old

* Party girl

* 5 feet, 6 inches

* Brazilian-Spanish Heritage


Biography

Twenty-three year old Marisol Castillo believes life is one big party, and she's always the woman of the hour. She is from the party hot spot and travel destination of Ibiza. She is very much a fan of electronic dance music of many kinds.

Personality

Marisol is a very laid back person. She loves the beach lifestyle along with the club lifestyle. She has lived in the United States in Laguna Beach, California, USA; and she has lived in Mexico in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. Marisol does not party too hard. You will not see her being very drunk or high. She also tries to maintain her fit figure.

Like Candy Watanabe, Marisol loves 1970s style. Marisol is a lot more casual than Candy and not as flamboyant as her. People have tried to manufacture a rivalry between Marisol and Candy. However, Marisol has healthy respect for Candy. Since Marisol been wearing bell bottoms before Candy found her dream pair of bell bottoms, some call Marisol as "the original bell bottom girl." All in all, Marisol fails to understand why people try to develop a rivalry that simply does not exist and simply does not have enough fire to fuel a proper rivalry.

Fashion

Marisol is proud of her 1970s style while also trying to stay modern.

OUTFIT 1:
Marisol's main outfit consists of a cropped denim jean vest, an underneath purple bralet/bralette, a pair of beige bell bottom jeans, and some chunky black sneakers.

OUTFIT 2:
Her alternate outfit consists of a ringer T-shirt tucked into some high-waist bell bottom jeans, and topped off with canvas sneakers.

OUTFIT 3:
Her third outfit consists of a stylish bikini and paired with some gladiator thong sandals.

Fighting

Marisol has gained proper attention because of her brawler style offense. Some liken Marisol to Lydia

Rivalry:

Marisol has no serious rivalry, but many liken Marisol's rival to be Candy Watanabe since both wear bell bottoms. Marisol has healthy love and respect for Candy. It seems more a manufactured rivalry. However, many others believe Marisol is equal in ability to Lydia, minus Lydia's shady techniques.

Trivia

* The name "Marisol" translates to "sea and sun." The Mari-part means "sea," and the sol part translates to "sun."

* Marisol's main outfit was originally supposed to be in her bikini while also wearing the vest, jeans, and sneakers. It was since changed.
