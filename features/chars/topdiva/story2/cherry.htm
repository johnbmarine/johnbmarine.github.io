Cherry Nguyen

* [Proper] Name: Quien Nguyen

* From: Saigon, Vietnam

* 19 years old

* race car driver, fashion model, and go-go dancer

* 5 feet, 4 inches tall

* Vietnamese heritage


Biography

Cherry Nguyen enjoys being a race car driver. She also partakes in modeling and go-go dancing. She goes by her English name of Cherry. She moved to Torrance, California, USA to expand upon her racing career.

Personality

Cherry considers herself friendly.

Fashion

_

OUTFIT 1:
Cherry's main outfit is her two-tone T-shirt that has a cherry on it. The shirt is white with red accents. She wears some beige short shorts along with some black thong sandals.

OUTFIT 2:
Her second outfit is her cherry red racing suit.

OUTFIT 3:
For someone who is named Cherry, Cherry would sound like a shoo-in for Candy Watanabe's go-go dancing team. Cherry wears a red dancing outfit with a cropped top and some bikini bottoms. Bright red platform boots finish her go-go outfit.

OUTFIT 4:
Cherry's fourth outfit is a traditional Vietnamese outfit- an Ao Dai. Her Ao Dai is a red dress with white pants and white sandals.

Fighting

Cherry is a speedy fighter. She has slightly less stamina than Tomomi Tsuchiya, though. Cherry's sandals have a backstrap so they stay on her feet. Even still, she is quite quick.
