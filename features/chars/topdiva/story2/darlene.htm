Darlene Espinosa

* Nicknames: Darlene Darling

* From: San Antonio, Texas, USA

* 29 years old

* lawyer turned fashion model

* 5 feet, 8 inches


Biography

After years training to become the best lawyer in the state of Texas, Darlene Ivana Espinosa decided to become a fashion model.

Personality

Darlene is a woman of class and elegance. This is unusual for a woman in her late 20s. Darlene is a lady who doesn't mind being wined and dined. She expects you to shake her hand firmly if you do handshakes. Darlene takes compliments, but she expects adults to act like adults towards her... even including young college students. Darlene isn't overly antisocial or any kind of drama queen, though. She also isn't a hyprocrite or gets under anyone's skin.

Darlene will jokingly tell you that she only look crazy.

Fashion

Her primary outfit is a blouse, some pants, and some red high-heel shoes. For fighting sake, she trades her high heels for ballet flats. Sort of her trademark is having long hair cover her right side of her face. She also wears red lipstick.

OUTFIT 2:
