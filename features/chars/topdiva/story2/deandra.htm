DeAndra Hayes

* Nickname: "Delicious"

* From: Miami, Florida, USA

* 26 years old

* professional boxer

* 5 feet, 7 inches tall

* Black American


Biography

"Delicious" DeAndra Hayes has earned her fame as a championship-winning boxer.

Personality

Despite her successes in sports, DeAndra is seen as arrogant, sarcastic, and very self-centered. She feels this attitude is warranted being a champion.

Friendships and Rivalries

Deandra has a rivalry with Darlene Espinosa. The rivalry stems from DeAndra's arrogant attitude and Darlene thinking she is an outright bully. DeAndra is inspired by Lydia Odelia Vasquez Escobar, but even Lydia dislikes DeAndra's attitude no matter how much she is praised by DeAndra.

DeAndra is a straight boxer. She has to change up her offense a bit to take on non-boxing opposition. So she incorporated some grapples into her offense. Despite this, she rarely uses her legs or feet for attacks.
