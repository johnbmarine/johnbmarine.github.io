Welcome! You have arrived on the Characters page of an all-female concept I have thought of.

Originally intended to be a series meant to be used for a fighting game project, I have put together this assortment of characters to exhibit their personalities outside of the fighting realm. So therefore, you get to understand each of the characters I have conceptualized for this project. It has been a long-running vision of mine. So while it may have put together within a certain year or years, I decided to not give real ages of how old the characters would be today. The ages and such of these characters are their ages as of the time of each episode/series.



SUGGESTED AUDIENCE: TEEN

* animated violence
* mild language
* suggestive themes
