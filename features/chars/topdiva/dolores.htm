Grandmaster Dolores

* From: Laredo, Texas, USA

* 59 years old

* Martial arts instructor, Head of the Diva State Competition Committee

* 5 feet, 8 inches tall

* Mexican-American heritage


Biography

Granmaster Dolores has devoted her life towards becoming Grandmaster. When the Top Diva fighting tournament was established, she couldn't be any happier to become the head trainer of the Top Diva finalists.
