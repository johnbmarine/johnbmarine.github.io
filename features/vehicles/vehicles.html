Automation Vehicles I Created

"Automation: The Real Car Tycoon" allows you to build and develop your own vehicles. While anyone can create a vehicle in this game, this game actually makes you THINK about your vehicle. For example, do you know what kind of engine will power your vehicle? What suspension setup will you give it? What body style and rigidity will it have? It makes you think about a lot of things. Once you build a competent enough vehicle, you can test it afterwards.

If you have "BeamNG.Drive," you can export your vehicles to utilize in the popular physics-based driving simulator. Or, you can do like most players do in BeamNG and simply wreck the daylights out of your vehicle. It is all up to you.

This section features stories on each of the vehicles I have developed for Automation. Click on each of the items to learn more about my creations. If a link is not available for a given item, it is either in the works or hasn't been released/updated. So have some patience.
