John B. Marine's Locations Section: Race Tracks

Welcome!

Start your engines- let's go racing!

I am a motorsports enthusiast. When I had the opportunity to develop a race track for any given game or game engine, I jumped in on the opportunity to develop courses. What you will see here are my many different creations.

Believe it or not, a lot of my earliest creations for race tracks were from non-racing games. So I was kind of like, "fake it until you make it." I still had fun with my work regardless. Now I am sharing my work with you. A hope of mine is to offer these for download or sale across a number of different platforms.

Items with "~content unavailable~" do not lead to anything because a content piece for that item has not been finished or updated. You can still click on any given item, but it will not take you beyond this page. Be sure to keep visiting this page for updates.
