Welcome to ABCs of Fashion!

by: John B. Marine

Welcome to my Series regarding the ABC's of Fashion!

This series features a number of different fashion-related topics, including some topics that are number-based. These are all my own opinions regarding certain fashion items, trends, and more. All commentary is of my own. Feel free to agree or disagree at will.

Here is how this series works. To get started, click on any of the buttons to see the individual fashion items or topics based on a certain letter or number. Each link you click on will access you to a variety of topics under that specific letter or number. You may not know what you will find under each item, so feel free to look around (and maybe be surprised!). For example, if you wanted to look up my thoughts on mary janes, click on the "M" button to see all items with that letter. If you are curious what fashion topics I discuss under the letter "D," then click on "D" to see all the D-related topics.

Sounds interesting? Go ahead and look around! Transparent buttons indicate a page that has not been completed yet or may be under editing. You could still click on them, but they will not take you anywhere.
