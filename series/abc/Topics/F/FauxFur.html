ABC's of Fashion: F

Faux Fur


DESCRIPTION:

Wearing fur can provide incredible warmth in cold weather.  However, there are those who feel suspicious for wearing garments or having accessories that come from killed animals.  So a way to provide guilt-free fur is through faux fur.  Faux fur provides the look and warmth of fur, but you can feel good knowing no animals were harmed in the creation of this fur.


THOUGHTS:

If you are conscious about the ethical treatment of animals or some sort of animal activist, faux fur is a good way to enjoy guilt-free fashion.  You will not have to worry about wearing items that were made from animals being killed with faux fur.
