ABC's of Fashion: F

Flip-Flops (or Thong) Sandals


DESCRIPTION:

Almost everyone has at least one pair of flip-flop sandals.  These thong sandals are characterized by their thong up front followed by the fact they flap around when individuals walk in them.  Many see flip-flops as the laziest shoes anyone can wear.  Though at the same time, they can be plenty stylish for casual days and casual outfits.
