ABC's of Fashion: F

"Fatshion"


DESCRIPTION:

Some plus-size/curvy fashion bloggers have coined the term “fatshion” to discuss plus-size feminine fashion.  Degrading as it may sound, plus-size fashion surely has its place in the general realm of fashion.  Many people are not as respectful about plus-size fashion.  A key element of plus-size fashion is in body acceptance.  It is further complimented by featuring fashion that fits plus-size females who may not have slim and slender bodies.
