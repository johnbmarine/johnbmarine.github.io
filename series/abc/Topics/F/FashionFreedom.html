ABC's of Fashion: F

Fashion Freedom


DESCRIPTION:

Why do females often get away with dressing up in male clothing?  Why is it that males who wear feminine clothes are criticized?  Fashion freedom suggests you should be able to wear whatever you want and not face criticism for your fashion choices.  So in other words, if you are a male who wants to wear a floral skirt or some high-heel pumps, you should be able to enjoy anything you wear.  Society says that males should dress like males, and females should dress as females.  Fashion should suggest you wear whatever you want.  You should be able to pull off any sort of look you want to go with regardless of gender roles or gender norms.  Even a male who wants to wear appealing lingerie should be able to wear such items if he so chooses.  Fashion freedom has so many intricate levels and connotations that should not go unnoticed.


THOUGHTS:

Even if people criticize you for your fashion choices, just wear what makes you comfortable.  Wear clothing that suggests your fashion style and what you are most comfortable wearing.  I have seen fashion bloggers and other male personalities look charming even wearing feminine garments and feminine shoes.  Males wearing feminine outfits should not be taboo or off-limits.  This element of fashion is such a serious double standard.
