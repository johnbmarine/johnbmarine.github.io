Fake Tan


DESCRIPTION:

Want to get tanned but do not want to commit?  Try a fake tan!  Fake tanning will allow you to have tanned skin without needing to stay in the sun for so long or get into a tanning bed.  If you are concerned about your health, at least having a fake tan means you do not have to worry about getting skin cancer due to radiation from sun rays.
