ABC's of Fashion: F

Faux Leather


DESCRIPTION:

Leather can be very stylish as accessories and garments, and they can also be very warm with garments.  A disadvantage to some people, though, is that feeling of guilt for wearing garments that involved the killing of animals.  Therefore, faux leather is created to provide quality leather style and fashions without the guilt of wearing items from killed animals.


THOUGHTS:

Again- if you are concerned about wearing items made from killed animals, consider faux leather if you still want the appeal of real leather but without the guilt of wearing something from dead animals.
