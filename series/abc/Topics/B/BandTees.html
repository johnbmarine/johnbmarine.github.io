ABC's of Fashion: B

Band T-Shirts (or Band Tees)

DESCRIPTION:

Band T-shirts (or more commonly referred to as band tees) are very popular for those into various music groups.  They have been common graphic T-shirts bearing the likeness of various musicians, usually that of any genre of rock music.  Depending on the represented group, band tees can be either a unique style statement or an example of edgy fashion style.  One will not be disappointed regardless if the fashionable person is into a certain group.

THOUGHTS:

Personally, I am not into rock or metal much.  While band tees are not exclusive to rock or metal artists or groups, I am not much into them to wear their likeness in T-shirts.  You can more than likely enjoy band tees either if you love the musician(s) in question or if you want something graphic and appealing to compliment your outfits.
