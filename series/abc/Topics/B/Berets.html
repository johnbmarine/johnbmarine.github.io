ABC's of Fashion: B

Berets

DESCRIPTION:

A quintessential element of Parissiene chic is a beret cap.  These beret caps are kind of like low chef's hats.  They are a very nice compliment to a preppy outfit.

THOUGHTS:

I think berets offer a cute touch to preppy style outfits.  I never saw a beret I disliked.  So feel free to wear these proudly!
