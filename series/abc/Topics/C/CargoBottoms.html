ABC's of Fashion: C

Cargo Bottoms

DESCRIPTION:

Need to carry some stuff?  Cargo bottoms can do just that.  You can carry a bit more items with you in these bottoms.  Style-wise, they can be quite tough and incredibly urban.  Cargo bottoms can include jeans, pants, shorts, and skirts.  Most cargo bottoms have two extra pockets for carrying more material.  Some cargo bottoms may even have more than two cargo pockets.
