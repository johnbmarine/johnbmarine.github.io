ABC's of Fashion: C

Candy Stripes

DESCRIPTION:

Candy stripes is a style that is similar to what you would see with candy canes.  Candy stripes candy… I mean, can be… stylish for various garments and accessories.  Candy stripes are not exclusively the traditional peppermint pattern of white with red or white with pink.  Some candy stripe combinations can consist of a rainbow of colors.
