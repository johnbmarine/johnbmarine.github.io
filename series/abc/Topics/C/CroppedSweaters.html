ABC's of Fashion: C

Cropped Sweaters


DESCRIPTION:

Sweaters provide great warmth on cool or cold weather.  Cropped sweaters offer the warmth of sweaters while also providing hints of midriff.  Some would find cropped sweaters to be foolish since you want all of your extremities to be warm on the coldest of days.  However, consider there are some fashionistas who pair cropped tops with high-waist bottoms.  Some even wear cropped tops with dresses or even layer cropped tops with regular-length or long-length tops or dresses.  So this is not entirely foolish a concept as some may make out cropped sweaters to be.

THOUGHTS:

I would advise against wearing a cropped sweater if it is too cold, especially if you are planning to go cropped sweater with bare midriff.  Some fashionistas would probably be best to layer cropped sweaters or treat them like shrugs.  Not at all do I dislike cropped sweaters.  It is just that they can be impractical to me for when it is cold.
