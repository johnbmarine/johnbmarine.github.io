ABC's of Fashion: C

Camisoles

DESCRIPTION:

A camisole is a feminine tank top.  Most camisoles are usually meant for lingerie and for lounging.  However, camisoles can even be stylish as non-intimate wear.  So a female could wear a camisole with jeans or a skirt as much as she could wear a camisole with some intimate bottoms.

THOUGHTS:

I surely no problem with camisoles.  They can be comfortable, airy, and sometimes flirty.  You can style a camisole almost any way you like.  No wrong style suggestions with camisoles.
