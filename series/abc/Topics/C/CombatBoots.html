ABC's of Fashion: C

Combat Boots

DESCRIPTION:

Combat boots are meant to do damage.  They are meant for the ones who want to incorporate toughness into their wardrobe.  Such boots are not for the “cute” types.  Combat boots are usually lace-up boots that are as high as mid-calf to about as high as knee-length.  They are seemingly the go-to style of footwear for those who (especially among females) feel the need to toughen up a dainty or girly outfit.  Many combat boots range in styles from solid colors to those with different colors, different patterns, and even some combat boots with intricate designs to them.
