ABC's of Fashion: C

Cropped Flare Jeans, Pants, and Jumpsuits

DESCRIPTION:

While bell bottom or flared jeans and pants can be groovy and cool, what can be equally fun are cropped flared jeans and pants.  For taller figures, cropped flares can nicely warrant being able to wear a wild pair of shoes.  Done right, cropped flares can be just as fun as your average floor-length flared jeans and pants.  An unfortunate negative to these bottoms is that they can swallow up the proportions of petite females.  In this sense, cropped flares can be like floor-length flared bottoms for petites.  In much the same sense, even petites in cropped flares can compliment these bottoms nicely with a wild pair of shoes or some platform shoes.

THOUGHTS:

I think cropped flares can look best when complimented with some wild shoes.  The kind of wild shoes I have in mind are usually platform shoes or some sassy pair of shoes.  I would mostly avoid something like an average pair of flat flip-flop sandals to go with these cropped flares.  Go ahead- try some wild shoes with these cropped flare jeans and pants!
