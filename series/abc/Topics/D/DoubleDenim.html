ABC's of Fashion: D

Double Denim

DESCRIPTION:

Many people view double denim as a fashion faux pas.  What the rule of double denim implies is that you never two or more denim garments together for an outfit.  Just like rules, many fashionable types break this rule.  An example of double denim would be pairing a denim jean jacket with any kind of denim jeans or denim jean skirt.  Some even have tried wearing a denim shirt with some kind of denim bottoms.  In addition, different color denim and different denim washes are put together to form certain outfits.  A female could pair a blue denim top with some white skinny jeans.  So to many within the fashion realm, the double denim look is really more a styling exercise rather than a fashion don't.
