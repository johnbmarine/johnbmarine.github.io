ABC's of Fashion: D

Dresses With Sneakers

DESCRIPTION:

Pairing dresses with sneakers, while not taboo, is a popular combination.  A dress suggests feminine charm.  Sneakers usually suggest casual or even sporty style.  Many fashionable females pair dresses with sneakers.  These dresses can either be very casual ones or even certain formal or chic dresses paired with sneakers.  Sporty dresses and casual dresses go perfectly well with sneakers.  Some fashionistas, however, dare try to pair sneakers with more chic and more appealing dresses.  An example includes pairing sneakers with bodycon dresses or slip dresses of various lengths.  For evening dresses, some fashionistas even try to pair such formal or ladylike dresses with sneakers.  Popularly doing this are celebrities like Rihanna, Gigi Hadid, Selma Hayek, Zendaya, and Rita Ora among others.  Some celebrities, however, have delivered some amazing looks pairing dresses with sneakers.  The one that sticks out to me best is when Kendall Jenner wore a white mini dress with black polka dots and paired them with some white Adidas Superstar sneakers.

In the sense of fancier dresses with sneakers, they are a way fashionistas make girly and chic dresses more casual.  For example, a sequin mini dress meant for a night out can be made into a daytime style by pairing it with a denim jean jacket and some sneakers.

THOUGHTS:

Certain dress and sneaker combinations work.  I just dislike how some dresses are paired with almost any kind of sneakers.  The balance of some dresses can be ruined with a pair of sneakers.
