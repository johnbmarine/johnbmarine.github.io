ABC's of Fashion: G

Gladiator Sandals


DESCRIPTION:

Gladiator sandals are very tough.  These are sandals that are very bold in design.  Such sandals have many straps and buckles to them.  Gladiator sandals are more for the fierce female who want open-air comfort in a tough pair of sandals.  In a sense, think of gladiator sandals like the sandal equivalent of combat boots.


THOUGHTS:

Say what you want about gladiator sandals, but there are very few I actually like.  Maybe I prefer sandals that do not have as many buckles and straps and stuff, but gladiator sandals just are not to my liking.  It will be rare I actually recommend gladiator sandals as footwear for an outfit.
