ABC's of Fashion: G

Gladiator Thong Sandals


DESCRIPTION:

If you know the usual style of flip-flop/thong sandals, gladiator thong sandals are thong sandals that are further up the top of the feet than a usual pair of flip-flop sandals.  More of the feet are shown off than with any average thong sandals with gladiator thong sandals.
