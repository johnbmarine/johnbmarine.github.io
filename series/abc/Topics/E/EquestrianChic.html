ABC's of Fashion: E

Equestrian Chic


DESCRIPTION:

If you are not familiar with equestrian chic, equestrian involves horse riding and various horse riding competitions.  Usual equestrian chic involves riding pants and riding boots.


THOUGHTS:

Among females, I consider equestrian chic to be a great cold weather style.  I usually think of a charming jacket paired with a warm shirt, some jeans or riding pants, and some riding boots.  The overall style is a stylish and comfortable one especially in cold weather times.
